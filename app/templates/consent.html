🎯 1. Nature, Purpose, and Duration of the Research

What is this study? This is a health data synchronization study that collects daily fitness, sleep, and heart rate metrics from your Google Health account to provide you with a personal health dashboard.

Purpose: To help you visualize and understand your personal health trends over time, including activity levels, sleep quality, and cardiovascular metrics.

Duration: Your participation continues for as long as your Google Health account remains connected. You may withdraw at any time without penalty.

⚖️ 2. Risks and Benefits to You as a Participant

Benefits:

  • Access to a personalized health dashboard showing your daily metrics
  • Visualization of sleep stages, heart rate trends, and activity patterns
  • Historical tracking of your health data over time

Risks:

  • Privacy risk: As with any digital health service, there is a small risk of data breach despite our security measures.
  • Emotional risk: Viewing health data may cause anxiety for some individuals.
  • No medical advice: This dashboard is for informational purposes only and does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment.
⚠️ Important: This tool is NOT a medical device. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical decisions.

🔒 3. Privacy, Security, and Data Handling Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption at rest: Your OAuth tokens and raw biometric data (heart rate samples, sleep stages) are encrypted using AES-256 (Fernet) before being stored in our database.
  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your device, our servers, and Google's APIs is protected with TLS/SSL (HTTPS).
  • Access controls: Only authorized backend services can access decryption keys. No human at Propsy Health can view your raw health data.
  • Data minimization: We only collect the specific health metrics you explicitly consent to (steps, calories, heart rate, sleep).
  • No third-party sharing: Your data is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties.
🔗 Google API Limited Use: Propsy Health's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

📅 5. Data Retention Periods

Your data is retained according to the following schedule:

  • Active participation: Your health data is retained for the entire duration of your participation in the study.
  • After withdrawal: Upon withdrawal, all your health data is permanently deleted within 30 days.
  • OAuth tokens: Access and refresh tokens are deleted immediately upon disconnection.
  • Audit logs: Minimal audit logs (without health data) may be retained for up to 12 months for security purposes.

🚪 6. How to Withdraw from the Study

You may withdraw from this study at any time, for any reason, without penalty. To withdraw:

  1. Log in to your Propsy Health Dashboard
  2. Click on "Disconnect" or "Withdraw from Study"
  3. Confirm your withdrawal
  4. Your data will be deleted and your Google access revoked

Alternatively, you can revoke our access directly from your Google Account Permissions page.

🗑️ 7. How to Delete Your Data

You have the right to delete your data at any point during the study:

  • From the Dashboard: Click "Delete Data" to remove all cached health records while keeping your connection active.
  • Full Deletion: Click "Disconnect" to revoke Google access AND delete all data from our servers.
  • After study completion: Even if data has been aggregated for research purposes, your individual records can be deleted upon request by emailing privacy@your-domain.com.
📌 Note: This study does NOT make your data publicly accessible. All data remains private to your account and is deleted upon request.
