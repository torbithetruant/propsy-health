Propsy Health Data Synchronization Study

Version {{ consent_version }}

Please read this document carefully before proceeding. It explains what this study involves, why it is being conducted, and what will happen to your data. You may save or print this page at any time using your browser's print function (Ctrl+P / Cmd+P).

🎯 1. Nature, Purpose, and Duration

What is this study? A health data synchronization study that collects daily fitness, sleep, and heart rate metrics from your Google Health account.

Purpose: To help you visualize and understand your personal health trends over time.

Duration: As long as your account remains connected. You may withdraw at any time without penalty.

⚖️ 2. Risks and Benefits

Benefits: Personalized dashboard, sleep and heart rate visualization, historical tracking of your health metrics.

Risks: Small privacy risk (data breach despite security measures), emotional risk (viewing health data may cause anxiety).

⚠️ Important: This tool is NOT a medical device. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical decisions.

🔒 3. Privacy and Security

  • Encryption at rest: OAuth tokens and raw biometric data are encrypted using AES-256.
  • Encryption in transit: All data is protected with TLS/SSL (HTTPS).
  • No third-party sharing: Your data is never sold, rented, or shared.
🔗 Google API Limited Use: We adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

📧 4. Contact

If you have any questions about this study or your data, please contact us at:

Propsy Health Research Team
Email: research@your-domain.com
Privacy Officer: privacy@your-domain.com

📅 5. Data Retention

  • Active participation: Your health data is retained for the entire duration of your participation.
  • After withdrawal: Upon withdrawal, all your health data is permanently deleted within 30 days.
  • OAuth tokens: Deleted immediately upon disconnection.

🚪 6. How to Withdraw

You may withdraw from this study at any time, for any reason, without penalty:

  1. Log in to your Propsy Health Dashboard
  2. Click on "Withdraw from Study" or "Disconnect"
  3. Confirm your withdrawal
  4. Your data will be deleted and your Google access revoked

Alternatively, you can revoke our access directly from your Google Account Permissions page.

🗑️ 7. How to Delete Data

  • From the Dashboard: Click "Delete Data" to remove all cached health records while keeping your connection active.
  • Full Deletion: Click "Disconnect" to revoke Google access AND delete all data from our servers.
  • After study completion: Your individual records can be deleted upon request by emailing privacy@your-domain.com.
