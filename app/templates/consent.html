🎯 1. Nature, Purpose, and Duration of the Research

What is this study? This is a health data synchronization study that collects daily fitness, sleep, and heart rate metrics from your Google Health account to provide you with a personal health dashboard.

Purpose: To help you visualize and understand your personal health trends over time.

Duration: Your participation continues for as long as your Google Health account remains connected. You may withdraw at any time without penalty.

⚖️ 2. Risks and Benefits to You as a Participant

Benefits: Access to a personalized health dashboard, visualization of sleep stages and heart rate trends, and historical tracking.

Risks: Privacy risk (small risk of data breach), Emotional risk (viewing health data may cause anxiety). No medical advice: This dashboard is for informational purposes only.

⚠️ Important: This tool is NOT a medical device. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical decisions.

🔒 3. Privacy, Security, and Data Handling Measures

  • Encryption at rest: OAuth tokens and raw biometric data are encrypted using AES-256.
  • Encryption in transit: All data is protected with TLS/SSL (HTTPS).
  • No third-party sharing: Your data is never sold or shared.
🔗 Google API Limited Use: Propsy Health's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

📧 4. Point of Contact for Questions

Email: research@your-domain.com | Privacy: privacy@your-domain.com

📅 5. Data Retention Periods

Upon withdrawal, all your health data is permanently deleted within 30 days. OAuth tokens are deleted immediately upon disconnection.

🚪 6. How to Withdraw from the Study

You may withdraw at any time. Log in to your Dashboard and click "Withdraw from Study" or "Disconnect". Alternatively, revoke access from your Google Account Permissions page.

🗑️ 7. How to Delete Your Data

Click "Delete Data" in the dashboard to remove cached records, or "Disconnect" to revoke access and delete all data from our servers.
